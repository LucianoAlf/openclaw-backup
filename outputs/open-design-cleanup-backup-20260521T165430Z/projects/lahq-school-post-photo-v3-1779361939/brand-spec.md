# LA Music School V3

Visual direction for the carousel:

- Background: deep charcoal with controlled pink energy, never bright or playful.
- Accent: electric pink for one focal action or pulse per card.
- Surface: near-black layers for overlays and caption bands.
- Muted: soft gray for supporting text and microcopy.
- Border: subtle gray, mostly for separation on dark fields.
- Highlight: white for logo, headlines, and CTA clarity.

Color tokens:

- `--bg`: `oklch(14% 0.01 25)`
- `--surface`: `oklch(18% 0.01 25)`
- `--fg`: `oklch(97% 0.01 25)`
- `--muted`: `oklch(73% 0.01 25)`
- `--border`: `oklch(34% 0.01 25)`
- `--accent`: `oklch(64% 0.24 5)`

Typography:

- Display: `Prompt Black`
- Body: `Prompt Regular`
- Emphasis / numeric marks: `Prompt Bold`

Layout posture:

- Tall poster format with strong vertical rhythm.
- Centered logo lockup at the top of every card.
- Heavy type over photography, with dark overlays for legibility.
- Minimal geometry: chevrons, outlines, halftone texture, and one clear CTA zone.
- No rounded marketing cards or generic UI chrome.
